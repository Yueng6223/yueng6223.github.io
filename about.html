  Welcome to Hijauan Group of Companies, a dynamic and innovative property

VISION &
  MISSION

The Hijauan Group of Companies recognizes the basic
need for housing and it is fully committed to produce
and provide high quality affordable homes to house

In 2010, Desa Hijauan Sdn Bhd (922925-V) is founded with a passion to produce and provide high quality and affordable homes.
